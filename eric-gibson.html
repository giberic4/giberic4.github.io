Eric Gibson

Overview

Applied software and systems experience in mission-focused environments.

Eric Gibson is a software engineer with experience supporting U.S. Air Force aerospace simulation systems in secure training and government-facing environments. His work spans software development, debugging, verification support, technical writing, and long-lived system sustainment.

He contributes across both code and operational delivery: implementing updates, resolving discrepancies, preparing systems for test events, supporting hardware and software integration, and helping teams move work forward in tightly controlled environments.
